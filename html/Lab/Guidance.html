GUIDANCE

This session will guid you on how to use the laboratory platform. For a start it is better to know how the laboratory is integrated with other parts of the program. The main partners of the laboratory technicians is the doctors. After the doctors examin their patients, the will send some laboratory samples to be eamined if there is any. It is the laboratory technichians duty to give full, specific and acurate explanation about the examined samples to the doctors mainly by the code generated for the users.

The lab technichians are responsible to the message they sent to the doctors about the examined samples. It is the way how the doctors finalize the patient's case. Also it is how the pharmacists give medicines for the patient. It is important to note that the lab technichians should be aware of the samples to be examined.

The other part in this lab session is related to the laboratory equipments. Laboratory technichians are the ones who will register the equipments. There are two pages related to laboratory equipments :

✔ The page for registering laboratory equipments.

✔ The other one is for examining the stored equipments.

Moreover, registering laboratory equipments is simple. First you should visit the store and there you will find the last equipment stored and from that equipment you can find its code. This code will help you to give a code for the equipment that you want to register. For the matter of simplicity we have managed the laboratory equipments needed by many lab technichians on the right side of the page. So select the equipment you want there and finnaly after you fill the inputs as required and then click ADD button.

The other important page as discussed earlier is the page for visiting the store. There you have access for performing different actions; for instance, you can visit the equipments you store and you can even create a pdf file for the sore.
