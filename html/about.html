ABOUT US

MAIRA CLINIC is a health care system modulated with simple community based insurance system. Health insurance is a contract that requires an insurer to pay some or all of a person's healthcare cost in exchange for a premimum. More specifically, health insuranece typically pays for medical expenses incurred by the indured. Health insurance can reimburse the insured for expenses incurred from illness or injury, or pay the care provider directly.

It is often included in employer benefit packages as a means of enticing quality employees, with premiums partially covered by the employer but ofte also deducted from employee paychecks. The cost of health insurance premiums is deductible to the payer, and the benefits received are tax-free, with certain exceptions for corporation employees.

These days, digitalized health-care system, keeping the best outcmes for patients at the center of all activity increasingly depends on the smart use of medical data.

Health insurance can solve the following current problems :

✔ Losing individuals insurance data: Having not digitalized system, causes insurance data of people to miss, this means if a person is in a condition which he cannot afford money for hospital fees, he/she can't get healthcare services. Thus, the person's health can be endangered.

✔ Slow data flow: The slow data entering and accessng in health centers can increase average time that the customers spend in queue. Consequently, patients who are in hurry won't get access.

✔ Income corruption: As the net movement of money in hospitals is not documented digitally and documents can be forged, there might be opportunities fr the collected insurance money to be corrupted.

✔ Limited health insurance providing: Some healh insurances may not cover beyond the diagnosis. Due to this, it will be hard for people, who are in a situation which they can't afford payment for the drug prescriptions.
